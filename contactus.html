Meet Our Anxiety-Reliefing Specialists

We are thrilled to introduce the heart and soul of our mission at Waggies - our four-legged ambassadors! These incredible dogs aren't just companions; they're partners in our quest to alleviate anxiety and promote well-being for both pets and owners.

These remarkable dogs aren't just ambassadors; they're pioneers in the realm of canine well-being. With their unwavering support, we continue to craft innovative products like essential oils for stress relief, paw wax, and calming soap to empower dogs and their owners on their journey towards tranquility.
Let us proudly present our ambassadors!

Lincoln
Meet Lincoln, our adventurous 7-year-old Norwegian Ambassador, who loves cuddles but battles anxiety triggered by mopeds and certain dogs. With Waggies' support, we've developed products to help him overcome anxiety and thrive
Flo
Meet Flo, our Dutch ambassador, a lively 4-year-old border collie blue merle. She enjoys agility, runs with her owners, and her best friend is Archie, her cat housemate. With Waggies, Flo's anxiety has reduced, making her a calmer, happier pup.
Tjibbe
Meet Tjibbe, our Dutch ambassador, a 9-year-old goldendoodle who loves eating, barking, and cuddling. However, he battles abandonment anxiety and barks loudly when left alone. Thanks to Waggies' calming oils, Tjibbe is more relaxed, easing both his anxiety and his owners' worries.
