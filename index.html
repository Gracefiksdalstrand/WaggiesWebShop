About Us

Waggies originated from the collaborative vision of four Creative Business students driven by a common goal: to alleviate the anxiety experienced by dog owners and their beloved pets. Witnessing firsthand the profound impact of canine anxiety on families and communities, the founders recognized an imperative need to extend empathy and support to our furry companions.

Consequently, the company's mission crystallized into a commitment: "Ensuring every dog's tail continues to wag." At Waggies, we are dedicated to fostering a culture of understanding and awareness surrounding anxious dogs, offering comprehensive solutions to address these challenging circumstances.

Lincoln the mix breed dog, Flow the Border Collie, and Tjibbe the Golden Doodle were our orignial inspiration for this cause and for the company, therefor have been named our companies ambassadors.
